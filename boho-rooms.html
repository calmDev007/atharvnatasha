Description of Room

Another spectacularly themed room for our guests to immerse yourselves – it is the Boho Bliss at our resort. It's a vibrant haven with pulsating views, which can easily accommodate around two to three people at a time. With a verandah facing the azure flowing rivers and a sit-out area to provide you with a quiet, cosy time, you can spend the best twilight here savouring the serene countryside.
