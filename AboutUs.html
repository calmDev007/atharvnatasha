About Us
Hotel near Mumbai International Airport. This new age hotel is strategically located near the Mumbai International Airport and a few minutes from the Marol Naka Metro Station. Close to key business and commercial offices on the Andheri Kurla Road, SEEPZ, and the Bombay Exhibition Centre/NESCO, Jio Convention Centre Mumbai. It offers direct access to the Western Express Highway and Powai. The Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) and Bandra Kurla Complex are also situated nearby.

This hotel offers 125 plush and well-appointed Executive rooms, Reflection Rooms and Suites, a 24x7 multi-cuisine coffee shop - Magnolia Restaurant - Zebra Lounge Cafe, a hip recreation bar - lounge, an eclectic pan-Asian restaurant - Republic of Noodles besides a - Pulse, a Life Fitness gym & and an upcoming Spa to keep you feeling fresh-as-a-lemon.

Not far from iconic destinations like Juhu Beach, Siddhivinayak Temple and further onwards to Gateway of India and Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, the hotel is within easy reach of Bandra West, the upscale entertainment and hub of Mumbai known for its signature restaurants and bustling nightlife.

Dressed in neutral hues and vintage-styled art, the hotel wears a nostalgic look accentuated by classic leather sofas, chic interiors and understated decorative lighting. Together with modern facilities, personalized service, and impeccable service standards, it transforms into the perfect place for upbeat business and leisure travelers to interact and to unwind.

The hotel offers over 880 sq.mtr. of contemporary meeting space, including large conference rooms and a business center, to add that extra zing to your meetings and special events.

To make your experience as rich and beautiful as possible, our dedicated team is at your service. So for any queries, special requests or to book your stay at Royal NestIn, please get in touch with us.
